The Founder

Sagnick
Gupta.

"Om Sai Ram — discipline of the body is discipline of the mind. We train both, every single day."

Sagnick built Om Sai Gym on one idea: that a serious lifter, a first-time member, and a HYROX athlete should all feel at home on the same floor. He started with a single location, stayed hands-on across every discipline, and grew it to three branches across Silchar without cutting corners on equipment, coaching, or culture. A fourth is on the way.

From programming the powerlifting blocks to sourcing competition-grade HYROX stations, Sagnick runs the gym the way he trains — with purpose, not just passion.
